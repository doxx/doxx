### Barrett Lyon

Creator of the [Opte Project](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Opte_Project) (MoMA permanent collection). Founded [Prolexic](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Prolexic_Technologies) (acquired by Akamai for $370M), BitGravity (acquired by Tata), Defense.Net (acquired by F5). Featured in the book [Fatal System Error](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fatal_System_Error) for tracking down Russian cybercrime extortion rings.

Currently building [doxx.net](https://github.com/doxx/doxx.net): a freedom of speech and privacy platform.
